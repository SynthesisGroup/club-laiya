Beachside Properties

This rising Leisure Tourism Estate is the perfect environment for the discerning investor or the imaginative homeowner who dreams of building a beach house or starting a resort business.

The Praia Coast

At the edge of Club Laiya is the wonderful Praia Coast that perfectly faces the beach horizon. The Praia Coast lots will house a number of establishments for tourists & property owners to bask in while enjoying every bit of the beach breeze & picturesque views of the sea.

Contact Us

The Club Laiya Avenue

At Club Laiya Avenue, lot owners can host the retail landscape of the Seaside District with shops and establishments that cater to tourists’ wants and needs. Never falling short of tourist traffic, this is a perfect site to cultivate local businesses. Ideally, a property in Club Laiya Avenue can host a structure that can reach up to a maximum of 10 storeys, making it the most in-demand spot in Club Laiya in the future.

Contact Us

The Copa Loop

Lot owners at the Copa Loop will have a good social experiences as they enjoy the proximity near the Plaza Laiya. This central square at the Seaside District will deepen your connection as you enjoy the scenic landscapes, especially when it is hosted by various gatherings and events.

Contact Us

The Kava & Aiya Boulevard

The Kava and Aiya Boulevard lots are at the heart of Club Laiya’s Seaside District. Featuring its highly pedestrianized walkways, establishments like cafes, retail stores, and fashion boutiques can thrive because of the freedom brought about by the street boulevard.

Experience the Lifestyle

Seaside District

Club Laiya perfectly joins work and play together; not only does it make living magnificent; it also makes working from anywhere in paradise possible with its collection of infrastructure equipped with technology.

Blue Roads

The roads in Club Laiya are colored to the core. These roads are colored to extend the calmness and vibrance of the sea.

Wide Streets

Walking in the streets of Club Laiya is family friendly as there is more than enough space to feel the surrounding area and safely explore the whole estate.

Open Spaces

Living in paradise is meant to be breathtaking. Club Laiya’s open space naturally allows you to breathe fresh air while you explore exciting activities and tourist attractions.

Designated Ped & Bike

Everyone is free to explore Club Laiya’s amenities and attractions may it be by foot or by wheels. A designated pedestrian and bike lane makes exploring much easier and safer.

Expansive beachfront

Just like any resort, the beachfront is at the center of attraction. However, Club Laiya boasts an expansive beachfront with a shore that is nourished with cobalt blue waters.
