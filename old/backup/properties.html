Beachside Properties

This rising Leisure Tourism Estate is easily the perfect environment for the discerning investor or the imaginative homeowner to build a dream beach place or start a resort business.

The Praia Cost

At the edge of Club Laiya is the wonderful Praia Coast that perfectly faces the beach horizon. The Praia Coast itself will house a number of establishments for you to bask in while feeling every bit of the beach breeze. For entrepreneurs, this coast is an ideal place to build their businesses and garner potential revenue from tourist traffic and site events because of its undeniable beauty. For those who wish to just enjoy the beach breeze, a residential building is just the perfect piece to enjoy that beachfront.

The lots at the Club Retail Row will host the retail landscape of the Seaside District, ideal for tourists to avail their needs. It is every entrepreneur’s dream site to grow their local business as it will never fall short of tourist traffic. The Copa Loop

The lots at Plaza Laiya will experience life like there is nothing incomparable that unwinding in a harmonious community plaza here you can see different types of flora in full bloom while interacting with your co-owners in the estate. The Kava & Aiya

The Kava and Aiya Boulevards are the heart of Club Laiya’s Seaside District. It is where you can build a highly profitable business such as cafes, retail stores, fashion lines, and many miscellaneous establishments because of the its center street boulevard with Blue Streets. These Blue Streets signify a highly pedestrianized areas with bike lane that will make exploring much easier and safer inside the Seaside District.
